Number Guessing Game Rules

  1. The computer will generate a random number between 1 and 40.
  2. You have 6 attempts to guess the number correctly.
  3. If your guess is lower than the number, the circle will turn yellow.
  4. If your guess is higher than the number, the circle will turn red.
  5. If your guess is correct, the circle will turn green and you win!
  6. If you use all 6 attempts without guessing the correct number, you lose.
